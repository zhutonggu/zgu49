About me

I have been an Assistant Professor in Economics at Peking University HSBC Business School, located in Shenzhen, since 2017. My main research studies firm behaviors and performance using production and financial data with a focus on market power and firm dynamics. Currently, I am also working on trade policy and its impact on renewable energy markets, especially photovoltaic industry. Before joining PHBS, I have done my Ph.D. study under the supervision of Roger Klein.
