About me

Dr. Gu is an Economist specializing in data science and econometric modeling of market competition, firm strategy and industrial policy, with particular interest in energy, finance and transportation sectors. Previously, Dr. Gu served as an Economist in internet companies such as Kuaishou. He also worked as an Assistant Professor and thesis advisor at Peking University HSBC Business School from 2017 to 2021. His publications have been seen on Journal of Financial Econometrics, Energy Economics, Journal of Cleaner Production, etc. Before that, Dr. Gu obtained Ph.D. degree in Economics under the supervision of Roger Klein.
